SOP – Daily Media Scanning

Purpose: To identify outbreak/public health-related news from newspapers and online feeds for Mumbai, Maharashtra, and India.

Total Time:
30–45 Minutes
Done By:
Assigned staff daily

2 Step 2 – What to Check (15–20 Min)
Only scan:
• Mumbai • Health • Maharashtra • City News • India
Look For These Keywords
Dengue Water contamination Malaria Outbreak Fever Hospitalized Virus Deaths Infection Epidemic Food poisoning Swine flu Bird flu Gastro
3 Step 3 – Verify Using AI (5–10 Min)

Paste headline/article into AI chatbot.

Prompt:

"Is this related to any outbreak, infectious disease, food/water contamination, or public health risk in Mumbai, Maharashtra, or India? Give short assessment."

5 Step 5 – Update Daily Record (Most Important)

This proves task was completed.

Rules

Include:

✓ Disease outbreaks
✓ Sudden increase in illness
✓ Food poisoning
✓ Water contamination
✓ Multiple hospitalizations
✓ Public health alerts

Ignore:

× Politics
× Advertisements
× Entertainment
× General awareness articles

Important
