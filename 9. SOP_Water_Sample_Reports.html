Standard Operating Procedure

Water Sample Reports

(Monthly Water Sample Report & Monthly Water Mail Report)
START
↓
1
Login to FWQMS Portal
www.fwqms.mcgm.gov.in/admin/samplescombinedreport.aspx

Login with authorized credentials

↓
2
Select Water Tab
A. Monthly Water Sample Report (Updated Monthly)
↓
3
Select Sample Combined Report

Displays four types of samples:

1. Complaint 2. Cases 3. Other 4. Surv
↓
4
Apply Filters
• Filter Selection: Any Type • Range: 5000 • Date Range: Select reporting period • Ward: Select applicable ward
↓
5
Click Apply

Generate the report

↓
6
Retrieve Water Sample Data

(Data includes Surveillance Samples as Any Type is selected)

↓
7
Compile Data

Transfer retrieved information into the prescribed Water Sample Report format/template

B. Monthly Water Mail Report (To be prepared on the first day of every month)
↓
3
Open Monthly Water Report
↓
4
Apply Filters
  • Select reporting date range
  • Click on Zone Points
↓
5
Retrieve Required Data

Collect the following information:

• Total Samples • E. coli positive samples • Coliform positive samples
↓
6
Enter Data

Transfer information into the prescribed Monthly Water Mail document

8
Verify Information
  • Verify selected ward / date range
  • Verify total sample count
  • Verify E. coli and Coliform values (for Monthly Water Mail)
  • Check for any missing or duplicate entries
↓
9
Finalize Report

Complete report formatting and ensure accuracy

↓
10
Save Report

Save using standard naming convention

Example: Water_Sample_Report_Month_Year

↓
11
Share Report

Share the finalized report with concerned authorities as per established protocol

↓
START
Note: Ensure all data is accurate and verified before finalizing and sharing the reports.
