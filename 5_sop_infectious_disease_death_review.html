SOP – Infectious Disease Death Review,
CIF, IPD Paper Collection &
Death Committee Analysis

Metropolitan Surveillance Unit (MSU), Kasturba Hospital, Mumbai

Total Time:
As per case (Ongoing process)
Done By:
MSU Staff & Doctor Team

Purpose: To ensure systematic investigation, documentation, medical record collection, analysis, and committee review of infectious disease-related deaths reported in Mumbai. This process helps in early outbreak detection, identification of unusual deaths, epidemiological investigation and strengthening public health response.

1 Step 1 – Death Notification & CIF
  • Source: MSU Death CIF Google Form / Notifications from Mumbai hospitals
  • Infectious disease deaths identified
  • TB deaths excluded
  • Ward MOH/Nodal Officer informed
  • Field investigation conducted
  • CIF completed and submitted to MSU
2 Step 2 – Collection of Hospital Records

Responsibility: MSU staff

Hospitals Contacted:
  • Dean Office
  • Medical Record Department (MRD)
  • Treating Unit (if required)
Mode of Communication:
Mobile phone
Official Email
Official Letter
Documents Requested

Hospitals are requested to submit:

• Scanned copy of IPD papers OR

• Hard copy of complete case papers

Including:

• Admission notes • Investigation reports • Treatment details • Progress notes • Laboratory reports • ICU records • Death summary

Document Submission Location

Metropolitan Surveillance Unit (MSU)
Kasturba Hospital, Ward 38, Mumbai

3 Step 3 – Medical Analysis at MSU

Done By: Dedicated MSU Doctor Team

Documents Reviewed:

• CIF from ward • IPD case papers • Laboratory reports • Epidemiological details • Clinical course of illness
Analysis Includes
• Timeline of illness • Clinical diagnosis • Epidemiological linkage • Travel/contact history • Laboratory confirmation • Treatment timeline • Cause of death assessment • Delays in diagnosis/treatment • Public health significance • Outbreak potential
4 Step 4 – Preparation of Detailed Note & PPT

MSU prepares:

  1. Detailed case summary
  2. Epidemiological analysis
  3. Clinical analysis
  4. Timeline of events
  5. Laboratory findings summary
  6. Public health observations
  7. Recommendations

A PowerPoint presentation (PPT) is prepared for committee discussion.

5 Step 5 – Death Committee Presentation

Presented Before:

• Death Review Committee • Public Health Officials • Epidemiology Experts • Clinical Experts (if required)
Purpose:
  • Review cause and circumstances of death
  • Assess outbreak/public health implications
  • Identify preventable factors
  • Recommend corrective measures
6 Step 6 – Final Comprehensive Report

After committee review, MSU prepares:

  • Final comprehensive death investigation report
  • Clinical and epidemiological summary
  • Recommendations
  • Action points for wards/hospitals if required

  5. Death committee review
  6. Comprehensive documentation
